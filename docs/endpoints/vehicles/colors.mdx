# Vehicle Colors

Returns a list of all distinct vehicle colours and the total amount of cars with that colour.

```jsx
GET /v1/vehicles/colors
```

#### Query Params
|Param|Desription|
|-----|----------|
|`startDate`|Retrieve results after the given date|
|`endDate`|Retrieve results before the given date|
|`dateType`|- `regInJersey` - startDate and endDate checks the date the car was registered in Jersey<br/>- Anything else - startDate and endDate checks the date the car was first ever registered|
